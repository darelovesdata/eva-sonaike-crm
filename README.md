# Eva Sonaike Luxury Digital Marketing & CRM Strategy

A comprehensive project showcase website demonstrating strategic luxury brand marketing, SEO optimisation and CRM implementation for Eva Sonaike, a luxury African heritage brand.

## Project Overview

**Duration:** January - April 2024
**Client:** Eva Sonaike Ltd
**Role:** Digital Marketing Officer - Strategic Growth Focus

### Campaign Achievements

- +34% launch week sessions growth
- +12% organic discoverability lift through SEO
- +12% dormant leads reactivated without discount-driven messaging
- +52% overall traffic growth (Jan-Apr)
- +24% conversion rate uplift (1.8% to 2.23%)
- +31% email revenue contribution

## Campaign Strategy Highlights

### SEO & Content Marketing

- Story-led product descriptions optimised for search visibility
- Heritage content pages developed for long-tail keyword ranking
- Organic discovery optimisation resulting in +12% discoverability lift

### Email & CRM Segmentation (Klaviyo)

- Audience segmentation by engagement level and purchase history
- Lifecycle flow redesign for optimal conversion velocity
- Dormant subscriber reactivation without discount-driven messaging (12% reactivation)

### Channel Strategy

- Instagram: Community building and inspiration-driven engagement
- Pinterest: Visual discovery and aesthetic-focused traffic
- Email: Lifecycle conversion and nurture sequences
- Facebook: Affluent demographic targeting and reach

### Brand Positioning

- Identity-led messaging focused on cultural authority
- B2C strategy: Premium storytelling for affluent cultural consumers
- B2B strategy: Professional prestige and brand elevation appeal
- Messaging shift from product-led to value-led narrative
