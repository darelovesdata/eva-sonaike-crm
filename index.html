Key Strategic Insight

Shifted from product-led messaging to identity-led storytelling. For B2B audiences especially, Eva Sonaike is a signal of cultural authority and prestige, not just a purchase.

Campaign Strategy

SEO & Content

Story-led product descriptions optimized for search. Heritage content pages developed for long-tail keyword ranking and organic discovery.

Email & CRM

Klaviyo segmentation and lifecycle flow redesign. Audience segmented by engagement level and purchase history. Narrative-driven nurture sequences built for reactivation.

Channel Mix

Instagram for community and inspiration. Pinterest for visual discovery. Email for lifecycle conversion. Facebook for affluent demographic reach.

Paid Efficiency

Only top 20% of proven organic content boosted with paid amplification. Targeted CPA reduction through data-backed audience segmentation.

Campaign Narrative

The launch campaign demonstrated that luxury brands can grow through identity-led storytelling and organic channels. Rather than relying on discount-driven tactics, we positioned Eva Sonaike as a cultural authority, strengthening brand equity while driving measurable performance.

The combination of SEO, email lifecycle optimisation and strategic paid amplification created a sustainable growth model that compounds beyond the initial campaign period.
